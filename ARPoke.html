AR 3D Pokemon - iOS App

My love for pokemon has not died down since the early 2010s when the first games came out. As such, I thought it was fitting to create an app that can display pokemon atop their cards. Or, in my case, my Driver's License and School ID
In the camera, the app scans its surroundings for planes identical to the ones that have already been registered in Xcode. Once one is discovered, the app places a 3D rendering of the designated pokemon atop the card.
